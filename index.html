TRANSDISCIPLINARY ECONOMETRICS & DATA SCIENCE SEMINAR

SINCE 2021

TEDS is co-organized by scholars from Chinese Academy of Sciences, Kobe University, Kyoto Sangyo University, Kyoto University, Nanyang Technological University, New York University, Otaru University of Commerce, Seoul National University and Tohoku University. We provide a platform for econometricians and statisticians to interact and engage with each other while sharing their newest research findings. We welcome researchers and students in different countries and research fields of Econometrics, Statistics and Data Science to join our seminar.
